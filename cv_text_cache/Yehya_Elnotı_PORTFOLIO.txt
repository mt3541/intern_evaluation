LIFELINE VTOL – Innovation & Invention Conference 3

Our team developed a modular VTOL drone with vertical
takeoff and efficient fixed-wing cruise, designed using 3D-
printed structures and carbon-tube framing for scalability

Dogfight mission developed in Python: I
developed a vision-based autonomous dogfight
mission for a fixed-wing UAV. I used YOLOv8
together with sky segmentation to track targets
from a simulated GStreamer video stream. By
calculating tracking errors, I controlled the UAV’s
roll and pitch rates via MAVSDK Offboard mode.
Real-time object detection and UAV control were
executed using asyncio and multithreading. I
simulated the mission control in the Gazebo
environment.
Kamikaze mission developed in Python: I
coded the kamikaze mission for the Teknofest
Fighter UAV, primarily using MAVSDK and CV2.
I then simulated the mission in the Gazebo
environment.
