European Hyperloop Week (EHW) is an international Hyperloop
competition that brings together university teams from around the world

Award for our electromagnetic levitation system.

OTHER

Microcontroller Systems – HIL Control
Experiment
As part of a hands-on microcontroller systems course, I designed and
implemented a control system using a Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL)
approach. The experiment involved developing embedded software on
an LPC824 microcontroller to control a continuous-time dynamic plant
executed on a separate computer. Control inputs and outputs were
exchanged as analog voltages, and the system was discretized at 1 kHz.
This work combined low-level embedded programming, peripheral
interfacing, and control-oriented implementation, focusing on real-time
execution, stability, and closed-loop system behavior.
